# User Personas & User Stories

---

## Persona 1: Daily Commuter

### Profile

- Owns 1 car or bike
- Uses vehicle daily
- Not very technical

### Needs

- Reminders
- Basic tracking
- Easy logging

### Pain Points

- Forgets service dates
- Loses receipts
- No history tracking

---

## Persona 2: Car/Bike Enthusiast

### Profile

- Owns multiple vehicles
- Tracks modifications
- Detail-oriented

### Needs

- Full history tracking
- Parts & mods logging
- Cost tracking

### Pain Points

- No structured logging system
- Data scattered everywhere

---

## Persona 3: Family Vehicle Manager

### Profile

- Manages multiple vehicles
- Tracks for family members

### Needs

- Multi-vehicle dashboard
- Centralized view
- Reminders

---

## Core User Stories

### Vehicle Management

- As a user, I want to add my vehicle so I can track its data
- As a user, I want to manage multiple vehicles in one place

---

### Maintenance Tracking

- As a user, I want to log a service so I can track history
- As a user, I want to record parts replaced so I know what changed
- As a user, I want to track costs so I understand expenses

---

### Attachments

- As a user, I want to upload receipts so I have proof of service
- As a user, I want to view past documents easily

---

### Reminders

- As a user, I want reminders so I don’t miss maintenance
- As a user, I want alerts for overdue services

---

### Dashboard

- As a user, I want to see all vehicles at a glance
- As a user, I want to see upcoming and overdue tasks

---

### Export

- As a user, I want to export my data so I don’t lose it

---

## Success Experience

A successful user should feel:

> “I don’t have to think about my vehicle maintenance anymore — the app handles it.”
