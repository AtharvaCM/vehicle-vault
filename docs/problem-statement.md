# Problem Statement

## Overview

Vehicle ownership in India lacks a structured, reliable, and user-friendly system for tracking maintenance, service history, and important vehicle-related events.

Most vehicle owners rely on:

- Paper invoices
- WhatsApp images
- Memory
- Service center records (which are fragmented)

This results in:

- Missed service intervals
- Poor maintenance tracking
- Reduced vehicle lifespan
- Lower resale value due to lack of records

---

## Key Problems

### 1. Fragmented Data

Vehicle-related data is scattered across:

- Physical receipts
- Service centers
- Insurance providers
- Personal notes

There is no **single source of truth**.

---

### 2. Manual and Tedious Tracking

Existing apps require:

- Excessive manual data entry
- Repetitive inputs
- Lack of automation

This leads to:

- Low user retention
- Abandoned apps

---

### 3. Lack of Smart Reminders

Most users:

- Forget service schedules
- Miss insurance/PUC renewals
- Do not track wear-and-tear items (tyres, battery, etc.)

---

### 4. No Ownership Timeline

Users cannot easily answer:

- What work has been done on my vehicle?
- How much have I spent?
- When was the last major service?

---

### 5. India-Specific Gaps

Existing apps do not handle:

- Registration number-based onboarding
- Insurance/PUC workflows
- Multi-vehicle Indian households
- Bikes + cars in the same system

---

## Opportunity

Build a **vehicle maintenance intelligence platform** that:

- Centralizes all vehicle data
- Reduces manual input effort
- Provides intelligent reminders
- Tracks full ownership lifecycle
- Is tailored for Indian users

---

## Goal

Enable users to:

> “Track, maintain, and understand everything about their vehicles without effort.”
